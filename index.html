About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities across India. Founded with a vision of equality and opportunity for all, we work tirelessly to bridge the gap between privilege and poverty.

Our team of passionate volunteers and professionals work on the ground to deliver impactful programs that create real, measurable change in people's lives.
